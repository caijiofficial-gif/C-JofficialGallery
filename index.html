My love,

Happy 2nd anniversary.

It's hard to believe that it's already been two years since we started this journey together. Sometimes I look back and wonder how I got so lucky to have you in my life. Out of all the people we could have met and all the different paths we could have taken, we found each other. Since that day, you've become my favorite person, my greatest comfort, and the place that feels most like home.

When I think about us, I don't just remember the big moments. I remember the little things that made me fall in love with you even more. The random conversations that lasted for hours, the late-night calls where neither of us wanted to say goodbye, the way we could laugh over the smallest things, and even the quiet moments where we didn't have to say anything because just having each other was enough.

I still remember the day we spent by the sea. I remember your smile, the sound of your laughter, the warmth of the sun, and how peaceful everything felt. It wasn't because something extraordinary happened — it was because I was with you. Looking back now, I realize that some of my happiest memories are simply moments where I got to spend time with you.

These past two years haven't always been perfect, but they've been real. We've had happy days that made us feel like nothing could ever go wrong, and we've had difficult days that tested our patience, our understanding, and our love. We've misunderstood each other, argued, cried, and faced moments where things felt overwhelming. But through all of it, we kept choosing each other, and that's something I'll always be grateful for.

Thank you for staying beside me through every version of myself. Thank you for being patient when I was difficult to understand. Thank you for encouraging me when I doubted myself and for celebrating every little achievement with me. You've loved me in ways I never thought I deserved, and I'll never stop being thankful for that.

I also want to take this opportunity to apologize for what happened during my graduation.

I know I made a mistake. I know my actions hurt you, and I understand why they made you feel disappointed and upset. Looking back, I wish I had handled everything differently because the last thing I ever wanted was to make you question your place in my life or make you feel like you weren't my priority. I'm deeply sorry for putting you through that.

You deserve honesty, respect, and reassurance, and I know I fell short that day. I can't change what already happened, but I can choose what I do moving forward.

I know that saying "I'm sorry" isn't enough. Words only matter when they're followed by actions. That's why I want to prove to you — not just today, but every day — that I've learned from my mistake. I want you to know that she's no longer part of my life. I've chosen to leave that chapter behind because what matters to me is you, our relationship, and the future we're building together.

I know trust isn't something that comes back overnight. It takes consistency, honesty, and time. I'm willing to be patient because I know earning your trust again is something I have to work for, not something I should expect. I promise to keep showing you through my actions that you're the person I choose, every single day.

You once told me that you weren't going anywhere, and those words have stayed with me ever since. They've given me comfort during difficult days and reminded me that love is about choosing each other even when things aren't perfect.

So here's my promise to you.

I'm not leaving either.

I'll choose you on the easy days and on the difficult ones. I'll choose you when we're celebrating and when we're struggling. I'll choose you when life is exciting and when it feels ordinary. I'll continue learning how to love you better because you deserve nothing less.

Thank you for every memory we've shared. Thank you for every laugh, every hug, every conversation, every adventure, every lesson, and even every challenge because they've helped us grow stronger together. Thank you for making me feel loved, understood, and accepted. Thank you for becoming the person I can always run to, no matter what kind of day I've had.

Even after two years, you're still the first person I want to tell whenever something good happens. You're still the first person I think about when I wake up and the last person I want to talk to before I fall asleep. You're still my favorite hello and the hardest goodbye.

I'm excited for everything that's still waiting for us — the places we'll visit, the sunsets we'll watch together, the dreams we'll chase, the traditions we'll create, and all the simple moments that will one day become our favorite memories.

I know we'll both continue to grow and change. We'll become different versions of ourselves as the years go by. But if there's one thing I'm certain about, it's that I want to keep growing beside you. I want to meet every future version of you, and I hope every future version of me still gets the privilege of loving you.

Happy second anniversary, my love.

Thank you for giving me the happiest two years of my life. Thank you for choosing me through my best moments and my worst ones. Thank you for loving me even when I wasn't perfect.

I love you today, tomorrow, and every 07/23 after this one.

Always yours,
[John Carlo "panget" Moresca] 🌷
